Why "Solent"?

Two meanings, one name.

The stay: A solent is the forestay cable that a staysail attaches to on a cutter-rigged sailboat. This project honors a multi-generational sailing family and countless hours on the water. The logo's diagonal represents that stay.

The strait: The Solent, between England and the Isle of Wight, is famous for its complex, hidden tidal patterns that challenge even experienced sailors. Our tools do the same for technology — bringing clarity and transparency to complex, hidden systems.

The method: AI-assisted development is in this brand's DNA. Every project leverages AI as a force multiplier, amplifying what a lean team can build and maintain.
