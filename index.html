Why "Solent"?

Two meanings, one name.

The solent is the forestay cable on a cutter-rigged sailboat — the line that holds under pressure. Nautical heritage runs deep here.

The Solent strait, between England and the Isle of Wight, is famous for currents that confound even experienced sailors. A fitting name for bringing clarity to complex systems.
